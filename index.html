1. Quadrilateral Properties & Formulas

Square

All 4 sides equal, all angles 90°, diagonals bisect at 90° and are equal.

P = 4a
A = a²

Rectangle

Opposite sides equal, all angles 90°, diagonals are equal and bisect each other.

P = 2(l + b)
A = l × b

Parallelogram

Opposite sides parallel and equal, opposite angles equal, diagonals bisect each other.

P = 2(a + b)
A = b × h

Rhombus

All sides equal, opposite angles equal, diagonals bisect each other at 90°.

P = 4a
A = ½ × d₁ × d₂

Trapezium

One pair of opposite sides parallel. Non-parallel sides are unequal unless isosceles.

P = Sum of 4 Sides
A = ½ × (a + b) × h

Kite

Two pairs of adjacent equal sides, diagonals intersect at 90°, longer diagonal bisects shorter.

P = 2(a + b)
A = ½ × d₁ × d₂
